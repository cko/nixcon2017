lecture: NixOS Security

Vulnerability Roundup n+1

NixOS is receiving more and more attention from users expecting up to date packages and timely security patches. Weekly "catch-up" issues were effective, but faltered after our datasource dried up.

In this talk we will explore the challenges and potential solutions around regular NixOS security patching, and how to join the famous "-distro" list.
