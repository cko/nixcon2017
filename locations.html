Hotel Recommendations

Unterfoehring, where the conference talks take place, is reachable via the S-Bahn S8 (suburban train). The S8 starts at the airport, goes through Unterföhring and continuous to the city center (Ostbahnhof, Marienplatz, Karlsplatz, Hauptbahnhof/Main Station etc ). Any other accommodation nearby those stations would also be fine.
